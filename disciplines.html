Here at Kerry Canoe Club, we cater for a variety of disciplines. Our members regularly paddle:

  • Whitewater
  • Sea Kayaking
  • Surf Kayaking
  • Journeying
  • Lake Paddling

Whitewater

Check out some of the rivers we run. Also, don't forget to take a look at the Canoe Ireland website at the river kayak skills awards.

We have a number of videos of us kayaking on the club Facebook page if you'd like to check them out as well!

Sea Kayaking

Take a look at the Canoeing Ireland website for Sea Kayaking skills award information and Coastal Navigation.

Training is very important in our often unpredictable Irish Coastal Waters.

Paddlesurf

View the Paddlesurf skills award informationon the Canoeing Ireland website for up to date information on skills.

Don't forget to check out Magic Seaweed for up to date surf info, as well as checking out the Onit webcams for Inch beach and Brandon bay.

Lake Paddling and Journeying

We also go on a lot of flat water trips and journeys on lakes and the river Laune, from our base in Killorglin. These are mainly in the summer months when there isn't much rain for the whitewater rivers, and also with beginners.
