Here at Kerry Canoe Club, we cater for a variety of disciplines. Our members regularly paddle:

  • Whitewater
  • Sea Kayaking
  • Surf Kayaking
  • Journeying
  • Lake Paddling

Whitewater

Check out some of the rivers we run. Also, don't forget to take a look at the Canoe Ireland website at the river kayak skills awards.

We have a number of videos of us kayaking on the club Facebook page if you'd like to check them out as well!
