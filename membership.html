Online Payment

Members paying for full KCC membership including Canoing Ireland membership will be processed on the Canoeing Ireland portal. You will need to log in to Canoeing Ireland to pay for your membership.

KCC Membership (without Canoeing Ireland) (€35)

Only select payment via this option if you already have Canoeing Ireland membership through other means. E.g. through work or membership with another kayaking club. The KCC membership fee is €35

Pay Partial KCC Membership (€35)

When selecting the payment link above, you will be redirected away from the KCC website to a secure payment page, hosted by Stripe, where you can make card payments for the selected membership option.


Beginners Course

Please email the club directly to book a place on the beginners courses. We will be in touch on how to pay once your place on the course confirmed.
