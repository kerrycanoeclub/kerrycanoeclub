Online Payment

For 2021, Kerry Canoe Club have introduced paperless (and cashless) membership. The membership form is all online, and the member data stored electronically adhering to GDPR guidlines. Payments going forwards will all be taken online using Stripe as the payment provider.

When selecting one of the payment links below, you will be redirected away from the KCC website to a secure payment page, hosted by Stripe, where you can make card payments for the selected membership option.

Please read the descriptions carefully and ensure you are making the correct payment amount. Speak to one of the committee members if you have any doubts about how much you need to pay.

Kerry Canoe Club Membership (€65)

Use this option to pay for the standard KCC membership. This includes Canoeing Ireland membership, which covers insurance etc. and is required for club trips. For 2021 the fee is €65.

Pay Full KCC Membership (€65)

KCC Membership (without Canoeing Ireland) (€35)

Only select payment via this option if you already have Canoeing Irelnd membership through other means. E.g. through work or membership with another kayaking club. For 2021, the KCC membership fee is €35

Pay Partial KCC Membership (€35)

Beginners Course

For those attending the beginners course, please pay via the below link. The beginners course cost for 2021 is €100 and includes; Kerry Canoe Club membership for the remainder of the year, Canoeing Ireland membership, and a contribution towards equipment, materials, assessment, etc.

Pay Beginners Course (€100)
