River Safety and Rescue (RSR)

At Kerry Canoe Club we put on regular trasining sessions for our members on the RSR 1 and RSR 2 courses. Checkout the Canoeing Ireland syllabus for each of the levels - River Safety and Rescue Training.

Being competent in RSR gives our members the skills they need to keep themselves and each other safe.

Kerry Canoe Club RSR 2 Training. Rescuing swimmers from open water with a throwline.

Kerry Canoe Club pool session at the Gleneagle Hotel, Killarney.

Pool Sessions

During the winter months we have regular pool sessions to practice skills including peer and self rescues (yes, that includes the infamous 'eskimo' roll) in a warm and controlled environment.
