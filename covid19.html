Starting back club activities


COVID-19 Pre-return Form


Big thanks to all the instructors and club leaders that attended the meeting this evening.

Further to the meeting the following procedures have been put in place and must be adhered to by ALL members:

  • A return to paddling/club activities form must be completed by all members.
  • All members must exercise personal responsibility for their actions when engaging in club activities.
  • Keys to the club house must be disinfected when being exchanged by key holders and members.
  • All equipment must be disinfected by the member that uses it immediately after a trip.

The return to club activities form will be available in the coming days. It’s great to be getting back on the water so enjoy and stay safe.

Brian O’Flaherty

Chairperson

For the most up to date information, keep an eye on our Facebook page for the most up to date information.
