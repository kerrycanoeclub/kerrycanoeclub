New members welcome all year round; see FAQ here. Wednesday evenings during the summer are a good time to join us, as well as the beginners course below, lots of our more experienced members come along and either join in, or go for a paddle in small groups under their own steam.

Beginners Course

For totally new kayakers, we offer beginners courses which run for 6 weeks between April and September. Sessions are usually run on Wednesday Evenings at 6pm, finishing around 8:30pm, but keep an eye on our Facebook Page for the most up to date information. These are run from our base in the Bianconi Car Park, Killorglin, on the River Laune. This is a great location where we can learn the basics, go on short journeys, and practice skills.
